Key Value of the Invention

Rural hosts have another operation to tend to other than their short-term rentals. They are ranchers, farmers, homesteaders, and the like. When deciding today's tasks, they do not need an overcomplicated, immobile system.

Cabin Kingdom solves one simple question, for BOTH guests AND hosts:
What's going on today in the Kingdom?

Which cabin needs what today?

Rural hosts often hire temporary workers, who may be there anywhere a few weeks to a few months. The faster these workers can be onboarded, the more they can do in their short tenure. The management system needs to be temporary worker friendly. Unlike at organizations that hire long term, it's not useful to keep track of separate worker accounts here. A single tracker page for all workers to view is the winning ticket.

This is where the dual purpose of Cabin Kingdom begins to shine. The same landing page is of value to guests and workers alike. Without needing to login, workers can immediately see the cabin occupation schedule and know which cabins need today's focus. For a more detailed look, workers can log into the cleaning page.
