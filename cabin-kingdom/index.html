Key Value of the Invention

Rural hosts have another operation to tend to other than their short-term rentals. They are ranchers, farmers, homesteaders, and the like. When deciding today's tasks, they do not need an overcomplicated, immobile system.

Cabin Kingdom solves one simple question, for BOTH guests AND hosts:
What's going on today?
